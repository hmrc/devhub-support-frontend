Give a team member access to a software application

  • Sign in to your Developer Hub account.
  • Select the name of the application you want to give access to.
  • Select 'Team members'.
  • Select 'Add a team member'.
  • Enter the email address of the team member.
  • Set their permissions level to 'Developer' or 'Administrator'.
  • Select 'Add team member'.
  • The team member will receive an email to confirm that they've been given access to the software application.
